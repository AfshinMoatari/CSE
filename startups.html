    At Copenhagen School of Entrepreneurship you will find entrepreneurs and startups from a variety of different universities, countries and professional backgrounds. Copenhagen School of Entrepreneurship does not have any requirements with regards to university or studyline.
    This is also one of our key objectives and we constantly try to emphasize and further develop an entrepreneurial culture where companies can utilize this diverse environment when turning ideas into successful businesses. Being in an environment which is portrayed with people from all over the world and interacting with them can be both a source of inspiration and a helping hand!
