    Open Wednesday

    Drop by any Wednesday between 14-16h.

    Open Wednesday is the "open house" event at CSE. At Open Wednesday, you can drop by and talk to our great CSE Staff about your idea - get feedback and plan your next steps on the road to turning your idea into a business. The CSE Open Wednesday takes place every Wednesday from 14:00-16:00. There is no need to book an appointment - just show up, and we will have an informal talk with you. The address is Porcelænshaven 26b, 2nd floor, 2000 Frederiksberg – See you there!

    Explore the future of student entrepreneurship in the north!

    Since 2015, CBS Entrepreneurial Day has brought together more than 5,500 entrepreneurial minds to share knowledge, network, get inspired, and to showcase the student startup community in Denmark. Our approach is simple and clear: Entrepreneurial knowledge should be free and available for everyone. This means that the event is free to participate in, and open to anyone interested in entrepreneurship. CBS Entrepreneurial Day is the largest student entrepreneurship event in Denmark, and will in September 2017 gather 4,500 students, entrepreneurs, researchers, business professionals, investors, speakers, and likeminded; to explore and share the latest research and trends within student entrepreneurship.

    STARTUP STORIES

    Listen to inspirational startup stories at Café Nexus!

    Once every semester you will get the chance to listen to start-ups tell their inspirational story about entrepreneurial topics and their rollercoaster journey from turning their idea into a business. The topic will differ each time and you will have an amazing opportunity to be inspired and perhaps even get some feedback for your own ideas! The event will be held in the amazing surroundings of Café Nexus at Solbjerg Plads. For more information and the specific dates please stay up to date with the CSE event calendar!
