    CBS STARTUP

    Supporting new innovative thinking and entrepreneurship

    CBS StartUp aims at accelerating the impact of innovation and Entrepreneurship in the danish soceity and Denmarks position on the global scene. CBS StartUp supports initiatives based on entrepreneurial mindsets with close connection to Copenhagen School of Entrepreneurship (CSE). Student entrepreneurs create and grow more than 100 new ventures from CSE each year. CBS StartUps aim is to support the growth and scaling of those.

    ENTREPRENEURSHIP TEACHING CENTER

    The CSE teaching center

    Our goal is to support practical entrepreneurship teaching at CBS by giving lecturers and professors access to the leading student entrepreneurship community and some of the best student entrepreneurs in Denmark.

    Are your teaching innovation and entrepreneurship at CBS and would you like inspiration and support to integrate practical entrepreneurship in your course, then please feel free to contact Karina Rothoff at kr.edu@cbs.dk
